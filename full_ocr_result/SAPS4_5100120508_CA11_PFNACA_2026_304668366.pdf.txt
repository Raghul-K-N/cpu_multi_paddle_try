How to pay your bill
1. Pay online through Online Bill Manager (OBM)
You can manage and pay your bills anytime, anywhere using the bank payment options available in the Online Bill Manager. Visit bell.ca/oBM to register
and then enable paper suppression for your eligible accounts under Manage option.
In OBM you have the option to choose from the following payment methods:
a. Bank payments
         One-time debit bank payments
        To make a one-time bank debit payment, choose Make Payment option from the Statements page.
        Pre-authorized debit bank payments
        To set up a pre-authorized bank payment, select Setup Payment Methods option from the Statements page.
2. Pay by Electronic funds transfer (EFT)
You can set up EFT payments offline. Direct your deposit to a specific Bell bank account and email your remittance.
f EFT is not set up for your account, send an email to eft.wire@bell.ca to request assistance.
3. Other ways to pay your bill
a. Online or telephone banking
To pay through online or telephone banking, use the Banking ID provided below.
Note: Online and telephone banking options may not be available for all invoice types.
o. Cheque payment
To pay by cheque, generate your remittance slip online in OBM. Select the Make External Payment option from the Statements page and
choose Pay By Mail. Then click Remittance Slip to generate a PDF remittance slip. Print a copy of this slip to mail with your cheque.
Refer to the detailed cheque payment instructions at the bottom of this page.
Important: We are in the process of phasing out cheque payment options. We encourage you to start taking advantage of other options available to you
for faster and secure ways to pay.
